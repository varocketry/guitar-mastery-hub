## PRODUCTION RECOMMENDATIONS

### Equipment Needed:

**Camera:**
- Smartphone camera (iPhone/Android) is PERFECTLY adequate
- Tripod or phone stand for stable shots
- Good lighting (ring light or natural window light)

**Editing:**
- Photos: Minimal editing (crop, brighten if needed)
- Videos: Simple cuts, no fancy effects needed
- Diagrams: Canva, PowerPoint, or public domain sources

---

### Production Workflow:

**Batch Photography Sessions:**
1. Set up lighting/camera once
2. Film all photos for 2-3 lessons in one session
3. Review and retake any unclear shots
4. ~2-3 hour sessions, once per week

**Batch Video Sessions:**
1. Similar batching approach
2. Film all videos for 1-2 lessons per session
3. Multiple takes until clean
4. ~2-3 hour sessions, once per week

**Timeline:**
- 2-3 sessions per week
- 3-4 lessons completed per week (visuals)
- **8-10 weeks to complete all visuals for Lessons 1-30**
