## üé∏ CONTENT PHILOSOPHY

### **Core Principles Applied to All Lessons:**

**1. User-Controlled Progression:**
- "Complete when ready" not "Day 1, Day 2"
- Realistic completion times (2-7 days per lesson)
- No artificial time pressure
- Adult learning principles
- Respects learner autonomy

**2. Shape-Based Learning:**
- Teach patterns, not isolated fingerings
- 6 movable shapes (E, A, D, C, G, triads)
- Reduces cognitive load
- Builds mental model of fretboard
- Prepares for barre chords naturally

**3. Comprehensive Quality:**
- 7,000-10,000+ words per lesson
- Theory + Practice + Application
- Real song applications
- Extensive troubleshooting
- Video resource recommendations
- Success criteria checklists

**4. Pedagogically Sound:**
- Spaced repetition built in
- Deliberate practice emphasized
- BPM progression (40 ‚Üí 80 ‚Üí 120)
- Mastery before advancement
- Encouraging, adult-appropriate language
